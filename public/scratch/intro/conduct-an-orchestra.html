Conduct an orchestra

In this project, we are going to create an orchestra that can be conducted by clicking the instruments.

1) Add an instrument sprite of your choice.

2) When you clic an instrument, make it play a sound.

3) Now make the sound play multiple times with some pauses in between.

4) Add a new instrument. When you click the new instrument, make it play a sound. Try to coordinate both instruments with pauses to create a melody.

5) Add the Microphone sprite and have it reproduce a random sound when clicked.
